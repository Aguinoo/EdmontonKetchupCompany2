About Our Company

At Global Ketchup Company, We take pride in bringing you the Highest-quality and Locally made Ketchup from Alberta's finest ingredients. Whether you're a foodie, a restaurant owner, a loving mother/father, or something that just loves a great codiment, our ketchup is made to enhance meals and satisfy your palletes from its rich taste and natural flavors.

Flavors for Every Tastebuds!

Classic Tomato Ketchup

Rich, tangy, and pertfectly balanced with sweetness. Made from tomatoes that are pertfectly ripe with a secret blend of spices, this go-to condiment is great for burgers, fries and everything in between.

Spicy Sriracha Ketchup

For those who love a lil heat! This zesty ketchup combined with the traditinal and local tomato base with a fiery kick of Sriracha heat adding a punch to your favourite dishes.

Smokey Chipotle Ketchup

A deep smokey flavor with a hint of spice, Made with real chipotle peppers, this ketchup is perfect for BBQ lovers who want a rich and slightly spicy twist.

Maple Bourbon Ketchup

A unique blend of sweet maple syrup and smooth bourbon creates a rich, indulgent ketchup and gourmet flair. Perfect for the grilling seasons and the sweet tooths.
